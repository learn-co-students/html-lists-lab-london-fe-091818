Recipe for making a grilled cheese sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
    • smoked
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 1 tsp of Marmite
Preparation instructions:
  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
  6. Photograph for Insta!
  7. Eat and enjoy!
